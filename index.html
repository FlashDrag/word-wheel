      Welcome to the Word Wheel Educational Game!

      1. Select a topic
      2. Spin the wheel to get a random word
      3. Guess the word by it's definition or click on a tile to reveal the letter
      4. Earn points:
        • at the start, a credit score is given that adds up to the number of letters in the word
        • for each open letter, one point is deducted from the credit score
        • for a guessed word, the balance of credit points, which is equal to the number of hidden letters, is added to the total score
        • if the word is entered incorrectly, the total score counter is reset to zero
        • if each letter tile is clicked, revealing the letters, the total score is not reset
